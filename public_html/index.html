About Me

I’m Kudakwashe Nyambe aka John the Artist. I am a Hip Hop Artist, Songwriter, Singer, Beatmaker, Brand Ambassador from Harare, Zimbabwe. I have 10+ years of music production experience (mainly Hip Hop and R&B/Soul) and I started consistently releasing my songs online as of the year 2024.
My music is mainly about uplifting, educating, and empowering and I also use my art as an outlet of what’s happening in my mind and the world around me.
